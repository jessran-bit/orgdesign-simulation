EcoTripp: Evolving Structures

Welcome to the EcoTripp Simulation

EcoTripp is a sustainable travel company that started as a small five-person startup. As the company grows, its structure, communication, decision-making, and coordination systems begin to face pressure.

Your task is to make organizational design decisions as EcoTripp evolves. There is no single perfect answer. Each structure has advantages and trade-offs.

Your goal: Help EcoTripp balance flexibility, coordination, accountability, and growth.
